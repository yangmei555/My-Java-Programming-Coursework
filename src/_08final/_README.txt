In this game, if you hit a big asteroid, you get 15 points, hitting a median asteroid, you get 10 points,
hitting a small asteroid, you get 5 points. If you hit a big asteroid, a red UFO will appear, if you hit
that UFO, you get 15 points. If you press "A", there will be a shield to protect you, the shield will last
for several seconds before it disappears. After it disappears, you can press "A" to get the shield again.
You can press "T" to fire a new weapon, whose diameter is very long so it is more powerful.
